Calum Buchanan

Email: calumjmb [at] comcast [dot] net

Areas of interest: combinatorics, graph theory

I am a mathematician, excited to be starting as a Visiting Assistant Professor in the Department of Mathematics at the University of Denver in September 2025. I recently obtained my PhD from the University of Vermont (UVM) under the supervision of Professor Puck Rombach. Research and teaching are both great passions of mine, and I took pride in my role as a graduate primary instructor at UVM.

My research is in combinatorics. Primarily, I work on problems in graph theory, many of which are extremal or algebraic in nature. I'm also interested in matroids, hypergraphs, and a number of other combinatorial objects.

Workshops
